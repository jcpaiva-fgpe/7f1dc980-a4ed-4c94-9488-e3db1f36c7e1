        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">The </font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">if - else</font></font></strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"> structure is a selection structure, in which a logical expression is evaluated and, depending on the result, one statement block or another is executed.</font></font></p> 
        <h3><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Syntax:</font></font></h3> 
        <blockquote> 
         <p><code>if (expresión-lógica) {<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sentencia1;<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sentencia2;<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; …;<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sentenciaN;<br> } else {<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sentencia1;<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sentencia2;<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; …;<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; sentenciaN;<br> }</code></p> 
        </blockquote> 
        <h3><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Functioning</font></font></h3> 
        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">If the logical-expression evaluates to true, the first block of statements will be executed. </font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">If, on the other hand, the evaluation of the logical-expression returns a false result, the first block will not be executed and the second block will be executed.</font></font></p> 
        <h3><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Example:</font></font></h3> 
        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">In the following example, the text "APPROVED" will be written in the event that the value assigned to the note variable is greater than or equal to 5 and, otherwise, "FAILED" will be written.</font></font></p> 
        <blockquote> 
         <p><code>...<br> int nota = 7;<br> if(nota &gt;= 5) {<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; System.out.println("APROBADO");<br> }</code><code> else {<br> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; System.out.println("SUSPENSO");<br> }<br> ...</code></p> 
        </blockquote> 
        <h3><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Exercise:</font></font></h3>
