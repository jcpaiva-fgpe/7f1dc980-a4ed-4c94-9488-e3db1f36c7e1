        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">The Particular Directorate of Traffic (DPT) is committed to making drivers respect speed limits. </font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Without going into whether it is for safety reasons, to save fuel, or with a mere collection effort, we now know that in addition to traditional fixed speed cameras, section speed cameras are being put into operation.</font></font></p> 
        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">The section radars consist of placing two cameras at two remote points on a road in order to check how long the car has taken to travel that section. </font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">If the average speed exceeds the maximum speed allowed, we will be able to know (although we have not seen it) that at some point along the way it has exceeded that speed. </font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">For example, if we place the cameras 10Km apart on a section whose speed is limited to 110Km/h, and a car takes 5 minutes to be seen by the second camera, we will know that its average speed has been 120Km/h, and therefore somewhere he has exceeded the speed limit although when passing under the two cameras the car was going at 80Km/h.</font></font></p>

        <h2>Entrada</h2> 
        <p>Cada caso de prueba consistirá en leer del teclado tres números: el primero será la distancia (en metros) que separan las dos cámaras, el segundo indicará la velocidad máxima permitida en todo ese tramo (en Km/h) y el tercer y último número indicará el número de segundos que ha tardado un coche en recorrer el tramo. Todos esos números serán enteros.</p> 
        <p>La aplicación finalizará cuando los 3 valores introducidos sean 0.</p> 
        <h2>Salida</h2> 
        <p>Para cada caso de prueba, el programa generará una línea, indicando si el coche debe ser multado o no. En concreto, indicará "<tt>OK</tt>" si el coche no superó la velocidad máxima, indicará "<tt>MULTA</tt>" si se superó esa velocidad máxima permitida.</p> 
        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">In the event that the result is " </font></font><tt><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">FINE</font></font></tt><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"> ", the points that will be removed from the license will also be shown, according to the tens of kilometers that the average has been exceeded:</font></font></p> 
        <ul> 
         <li><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">0 tens of kilometers on average overcome: 0 points</font></font></li> 
         <li><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">1 ten kilometers covered: 2 points</font></font></li> 
         <li><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">2 tens of kilometers overcome: 4 points</font></font></li> 
         <li><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">3 tens of kilometers overcome: 7 points</font></font></li> 
         <li><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">of 4 tens of kilometers surpassed onwards: 10 points</font></font></li> 
        </ul>
