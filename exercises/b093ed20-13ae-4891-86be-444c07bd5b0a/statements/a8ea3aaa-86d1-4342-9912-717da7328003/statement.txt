        <h3><code>public int getButacasLibres()</code></h3> 
        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">It will return the result of subtracting the occupied seats from the maximum capacity. </font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">To do this, we will not directly access the </font></font><em><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">capacity</font></font></em><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"> and </font></font><em><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">seatsOccupied</font></font></em><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"> properties , but through the </font></font><code>getAforo()</code><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">and methods </font></font><code>getButacasOcupadas()</code><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">.</font></font></p> 
        <h3><code>public double getPorcentajeOcupacion()</code></h3> 
        <p><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">It will return the result of multiplying the number of occupied seats by 100 and dividing it by the maximum capacity.</font></font></p>
